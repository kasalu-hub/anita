I tried to ignore it.
Pretend it was nothing.

But some people don’t enter your life quietly.
They shift it.

You are not loud.
Yet somehow… you are everywhere.

In my thoughts.
In my plans.
In the spaces I didn’t know were empty.

I don’t want to call this a crush.
I don’t want to call it a moment.

I want to call it intentional.

You just made this dangerously unforgettable.
